## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need RC Lite if I already have full Rapid Cortex?
**A:** No. Full Rapid Cortex subscriptions (Essential/Professional/Command) include API access at no additional charge. RC Lite is for partners/vendors who need API-only access without the dashboard.

### Q: Can I upgrade from RC Lite to full Rapid Cortex later?
**A:** Yes. Your API integration remains the same. You add dashboard access by upgrading to Essential/Professional/Command. Any months paid for RC Lite can be credited toward first year of full subscription.

### Q: What if I need more API calls than my tier includes?
**A:** You'll be charged overage rates (see pricing above). If you consistently exceed limits, we recommend upgrading to next tier for better value.

### Q: Do you offer free trials?
**A:** Yes. 30-day free trial of Development tier (sandbox only). Contact sales to request trial access.

### Q: Can I get discounted pricing for non-profit or small agencies?
**A:** Yes. Non-profit PSAPs and agencies serving populations under 25,000 qualify for 20% discount on Tiers 1-3. Contact sales for eligibility.

### Q: What payment methods do you accept?
**A:** ACH transfer, wire transfer, credit card (Visa/Mastercard/Amex), purchase orders for government entities.

### Q: Is there a setup fee?
**A:** No setup fee for standard tiers. Custom development and advanced integration packages have separate one-time fees (see Add-On Services).

### Q: What happens if I exceed rate limits?
**A:** Requests are throttled (HTTP 429 response). No additional charges for hitting rate limits. Upgrade tier if you need higher limits.

### Q: Can I pause my subscription?
**A:** Yes, with 30-day notice. Minimum commitment is 1 month for monthly plans, 12 months for annual contracts.

### Q: Do you offer custom/enterprise pricing?
**A:** Yes. For unique requirements, volume deployments, or special terms, contact sales for custom quote.
